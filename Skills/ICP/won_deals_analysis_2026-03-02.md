| **Sales Director / Commercial Manager** | Claudio Fiore (Responsabile Commerciale), Federico Castronovo (Sales Ops) | Real Estate and Education |

| **Country Manager / VP** | Astudy Italy (Country Mgr), Gattinoni (Vicepresidente) | Key for international / multi-location businesses |

### Key persona insight: CRM roles are internal champions
Contacts with "CRM" in their title appear often — they are the ones who discover Spoki, run the trial, and push for purchase. They are NOT decision makers but are high-value leads for demos and sequences. Their exec (CEO/CMO) signs.

### Persona for outbound cold email targeting:
- **Primary decision maker:** CEO / Founder / CMO / Direttore Marketing → pitch business impact
- **Champion to activate:** CRM Manager / Ecommerce Manager / Digital Marketing Manager → pitch product + use case

## 8. What to Do Next — Campaign Implications

### Add to ICP:
1. **Pet retail / D2C** — 3 wins, fast cycle, high value (Zampa, ILMA PET, Cuori)
2. **Education** (academies, language schools, e-learning) — consistently large deals across Italy and Spain
3. **Fashion/Apparel Ecommerce** — Nuna Lie, Caleffi, Il Lenzuolo all won

### Prioritize for Outbound:
1. **Real Estate (Spain + Italy)** — Tempocasa (12 days close), Percent (organic Spain)
2. **Travel/Tourism mid-market** — Gattinoni proves it works (long cycle but large)
3. **Education with CRM/Marketing roles** — Albali from Lemlist, MLA World

### Copy angles that are proven:
- **Competitor displacement**: "You're on [Charles/Zendesk/ManyChat] — here's why Spoki is faster, simpler, or better priced"
- **Ecommerce specific**: "Senior Ecommerce Managers" care about cart recovery + post-purchase — proven by Caleffi
- **Spain campaigns**: Use Spanish-language sequences. Lemlist is already proven as the channel.

### Persona targeting refinement:
- For SME outreach: target **CEO / Founder** directly — they sign
- For mid-market: target **CRM Manager + Marketing Director** as a two-touch play (champion + exec)
- For D2C/Ecommerce: target **Ecommerce Manager / Digital Marketing Manager**
- Avoid: Social Media Manager as primary target (too junior, high bounce)
